# Stella's Kitchen 🍳

A cute dress-up + cooking game made by Stella (age 7) and her dad.

## How to play
1. Open `index.html` in any web browser (works great on a tablet too).
2. Tap the green **Play** button on the store door.
3. Pick your animal: **Cat, Dog, Bunny, Bear, Panda, or Koala**.
4. Dress it up with a **Dress, Shirt, Bottoms, or Shoes** in any color. Tap the **✕** to take something off.
5. Tap **Done** to see your animal all dressed up!

Use the 🎵 **music button** in the top-right corner to turn the background music on or off.
